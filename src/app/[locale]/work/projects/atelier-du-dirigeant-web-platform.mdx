## Overview

Web platform designed for L'Atelier du Dirigeant to connect business leaders with outsourced secretaries within their network, enabling efficient task management and invoicing between service providers and their clients.

## Key Features

-   **Collaborative Workspace**: Enhanced collaboration between business leaders and secretaries with shared task management.
-   **Administrative Automation**: Streamlined administrative processes, reducing manual work and paperwork.
-   **Order Tracking**: Precise tracking system for orders with detailed activity logging and time tracking.
-   **Invoicing System**: Automated generation of invoices based on completed tasks and tracked time.
-   **User-Friendly Interface**: Responsive design optimized for both desktop and mobile use.

## Technologies Used

-   **React**: For building a dynamic and responsive front-end interface.
-   **NestJS**: For creating a robust and scalable back-end API.
-   **GraphQL**: For efficient data querying and manipulation.
-   **MySQL**: For reliable and structured data storage.
-   **Docker**: For consistent development and deployment environments.
-   **CI/CD**: Automated deployment pipeline for continuous integration and delivery.

## Challenges and Learnings

A significant challenge was designing a system that could accommodate the diverse needs of both business leaders and secretaries while maintaining a simple, intuitive interface. The solution involved extensive user research and iterative design processes, with regular feedback sessions with stakeholders. The project reinforced the importance of user-centered design and agile development methodologies in creating successful business applications.

## Outcome

The platform successfully launched in 2023 and has since grown from 10 to over 40 users within a year. It maintains a 99% availability rate with support response times under 2 hours. The system has been fully monetized, providing L'Atelier du Dirigeant with a scalable solution for connecting business leaders with their network of outsourced secretaries, while automating administrative tasks and improving overall efficiency.
